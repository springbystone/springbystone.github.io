Madeleine Teh

Designer & writer based in Manila, PH. Interested in the relationships between technology and visual culture.

She currently works at NextPay YC W21 as a brand designer. Previously, she held roles at Canva, Silverlens, and the RISD Media Group.

She graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Art History from RISD.

Madeleine also writes about tech & art. Her essay on Instagram & vulnerability was shortlisted for the Purita Kalaw-Ledesma Award for Art Criticism at the Ateneo Art Awards 2023.

She also writes a fortnightly Substack on Internet culture, Zero Percent Sugar.
