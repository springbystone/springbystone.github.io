Madeleine Teh

Designer based in Manila, PH. Interested in the relationships between technology and visual culture.

She currently works at NextPay YC W21 and previously held roles at Canva, Silverlens, and the RISD Media Group.

She graduated with a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Art History from RISD.

In her spare time, Madeleine writes about tech and art on her Substack, Zero Percent Sugar.
